AI Twin Widget Test Page

This is a sample website that demonstrates the AI Twin widget integration.

How to use:
  1. Replace YOUR_TWIN_UUID in the script tag below with your actual Twin UUID
  2. Replace http://localhost:8000 with your actual domain
  3. The floating chat button should appear in the bottom-right corner
  4. Click it to open the chat widget and ask questions
